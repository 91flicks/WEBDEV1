Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until fully mixed.
  2. Heat the pan: Warm a non-stick pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Pour in the eggs and tilt the pan to spread evenly.
  4. Add fillings: When the edges set, add fillings over one half.
  5. Fold and serve: Fold the omelette and cook another minute, then serve.
