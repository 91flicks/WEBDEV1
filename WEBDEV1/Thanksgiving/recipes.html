Classic Thanksgiving Stuffing

Prep Time: 30 mins

Ingredients

  • Bread cubes
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Chicken broth
  • Butter
  • Sage
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Sauté the vegetables in butter.
  2. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl.
  3. Bake at 375 for 40 minutes.

Pumpkin Pie

Prep Time: 20 mins

Ingredients

  • Pumpkin purée
  • Evaporated milk
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Pie crust
  • Cinnamon
  • Nutmeg

Steps

  1. Mix all filling ingredients together.
  2. Pour into pie crust.
  3. Bake at 425°F for 15 minutes, then 350°F for 40 minutes.
